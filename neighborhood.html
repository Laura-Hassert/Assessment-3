Welcome to the Neighborhood!


Here in Downingtown, we take pride in our outdoor areas and have some of the best small businesses and restaurants outside of Philadelphia! Look out West Chester and Exton... Downingtown is moving up!

Parks

  1. Kerr Park

    Great, open park with lots of grassy areas to picnic, basketball courts and a skateboard area for the kids, and a great view of the pond!


  2. Lloyd Park

    The best dog park in town! You can let your dog off-leash in this completely fenced in area complete with multiple areas to play, train, and swim! Includes a dog washing station to clean your pup before heading home!


Restaurants

  1. Pomodoro

    If you LOVE Italian food, this one's for you!


  2. Station Taproom

    If American food is your jam, check us out!


  3. Victory Brewing

    If you love supporting your local breweries, stop in here!


  4. Estrella Taco Bar

    For the best tacos in town, swing by here!
